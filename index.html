You’re Invited: Research Study on AI Integration in P&C Insurance

You are invited to participate in a research study being conducted for a dissertation at Swiss School of Business and Management.

About the Research

The study is interested in your thoughts and opinions about how U.S.-based Property & Casualty (P&C) insurance companies are integrating Artificial Intelligence into their operations, with a particular focus on leadership strategy and continuous improvement.

Why You Were Contacted

Your firm was selected because it satisfies the main categories of criteria:

  • Property & Casualty insurance company or InsurTech company
  • U.S.-based
  • Known to have integrated or is in the process of integrating Artificial Intelligence

Individuals in leadership roles, underwriting roles, and IT roles involved in transformation initiatives will be provided questionnaires addressing the research topic.

Your Rights and Privacy

Although there are no known risks in this study, you can choose not to answer any question that you feel uncomfortable answering. The data collected in this study is confidential. Your name or personal information is not linked to data. Only the researcher in this study will see the data. You have the right to withdraw from the study at any time without penalty.

Purpose of the Research

There are no direct benefits to you of participating in this research. No incentives are offered. The results will have applied interest that may eventually have benefits for your company and other P&C insurance companies considering integrating AI into their practices.
